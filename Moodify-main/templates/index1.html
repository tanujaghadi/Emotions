EmoTune

Welcome to EmoTune, where music meets facial expressions in a wild dance of recommendations! Our Python-powered system reads your emotions and curates the perfect playlist just for you.

Say goodbye to boring playlists and hello to a musical journey based on your every smile, frown, and eyebrow raise. Get ready to groove with Emotion Beats!

Let your expressions guide the music, and let the beats reflect your mood. It's time to experience music in a whole new light.

How it work?

It can detect 7 emotions. The project works by getting live video feed from web cam, pass it through the model to get a prediction of emotion. Then according to the emotion predicted, the app will fetch playlist of songs from Spotify through spotipy wrapper and recommend the songs by displaying them on the screen.

Features
  • Real time expression detection and song recommendations.
  • Playlists fetched from Spotify using API.
  • Html ,Css and Bootstrap for UI Design
Problem Definition
  • Using traditional music players,a user had to manually browse through his playlist and select songs that would soothe his mood and emotional experience.
Purpose
  • Human face plays an important role in extraction of individual's emotional state .
  • Manually segregating the list of songs and generating an appropriate playlist based on an individual's emotions is tedious
